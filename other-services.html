Other Service

Please contact the office for Buzz set up, lost keys, elevator problems, carpet cleaning or anything you are not comfortable with. We are waiting to hear from you. Members can file any complain using their Member Log in IDs.

Information for New Home Owners

Congratulations for being one of our honorable members. Please contact the office for HOA payments, Storage, Locker, Swimming Pool and Entrance keys, and setting up the Buzz. You can contact National Grid for your electricity account and contact the Comcast for your cable/internet/home phone. IHOA covers Master policy but does not offer any insurance for your interiors. You can find a list of resources for new Home Owners here,

    Title Insurance
    Condo Questionnaire
    Independence Condominium Master Policy
