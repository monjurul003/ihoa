Waste Management

We have agreement for waste collection with Troup Waste Management services. They will pick up all waste on Mondays and Thursdays. Please place your recyclable materials in designate bins. For large items like furniture and electronics, please contact the office.

Snow Removal

During winter months snow removal operations will be going on after each snowfall in the parking lot. Large containers of sand and snow melting salts have been placed in every entrance. Please make sure you are receiving the notifications.
