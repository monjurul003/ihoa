Independence Home Owner’s Association

Independence Home Owner’s Association (IHOA) was established in 1969 to serve the 80 condominium owners of 195 and 205 Independence Ave regarding HOA fees, New Assessment fees, Maintenance works of the establishments, informing members with updated policies and providing services 24/7. Our members are our first priority and we always try to meet their demands within shortest possible of time. For HOA payment, Special Assessments, Annual Report and filing any complain members are requested to login by using their Member IDs.
